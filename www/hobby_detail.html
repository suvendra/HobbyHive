Myhobbyhive.com is a new online platform for all hobby enthusiasts interested in managing the activities of an existing club or individuals interested in connecting with others who share a specific hobby interest. It provides all the tools clubs need to successfully promote and manage the group – from membership list management and electronic newsletters to member chat and online club stores. And our club management resources always are FREE.
For individuals searching for a new hobby, or even looking to grow their skill level through coaching or mentoring with others, myhobbyhive.com provides a searchable database of hobby experts and individual profiles and clubs. The myhobbyhive.com tagline “Have a Hobby. Find a Hobby” encompasses our desire to provide resources that allow club members to communicate with existing members and reach out to prospects, and the myhobbyhive.com platform is integrated with social media tools to facilitate sharing documents, photos, videos and more. Clubs also can participate in revenue-sharing opportunities and sell products related to their specific hobby through setting up an online store.
